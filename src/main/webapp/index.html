js-upload-manager

A WebDAV browser / upload manager written in entirely in JavaScript. You can:

  1. Click on folders to browse around
  2. Upload image files (please, keep it SFW!)
  3. Images will be resized, then uploaded in chunks
  4. Click on images to view them
  5. You can browse away and come back during upload, and the upload will resume
  6. You can select files with the checkbox and delete them
This site will be up until it gets abused or until I get bored with the concept. Have fun!
